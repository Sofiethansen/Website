About Copenhagen Events

Welcome to Copenhagen Events — a place where people in Copenhagen can discover creative events and connect with others who share the same interests and hobbies.

What can you do here?

Browse upcoming events in Copenhagen, from photography walks to jazz sessions and art evenings. Join events that interest you and meet new people in your city.

Create your own event

Have an idea for a meetup? Create your own event and invite others to join. Whether it's a running group, a knitting circle or a music jam — everyone is welcome.

How to get started

Sign up for a free account, browse the events on the front page, and click on any event to see more details and join. It's that simple!
